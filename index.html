Christian Ignatius Latrobe's Voyage to South Africa
1815 - 1816

In 1815 Christian Ignatius Latrobe, a Reverend of the Moravian Church in England sets off to South Africa. Send to search for new places for more missionary settlements to come. The first map shows the way the Albion, a brigg on which Latrobe travled. The second map zooms in on his stay in Gnadenthal (today known as Genadendal) and his explorations around the settlement. The third map shows Latrobe's actual exploration of the south african "bushland" from Genadendal all the way up to a point behind Uitenhagen.
